SnapNostr

What is SnapNostr?

SnapNostr is an open-source application to make your Nostr notes look great!

How it works?

  1. Paste bech32 note ID or URLs of note from platforms like Primal, Damus, Iris and Snort
  2. Customize the background color scheme
  3. Adjust the image size based on your idea
  4. And just hit download!
  5. Alternatively, you can copy the image and paste it anywhere.

Looks familiar?

Yes, it is inspired by poet.so!
